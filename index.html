Approaching to a Renewable Target
HyChar Energy, LLC is founded by a group of scientists from MIT, Harvard and URI providing hydrogen and clean technologies. We have developed a novel hydrogen storage technology for fuel cell.

By this technology, the concentrated anions of hydrogen produced by a novel anion-processor, could be absorbed into activated carbon as a safe hydrogen storage in low pressure for fuel cell applications. Also, a novel reactor method could also be used to produce ethanol fuel and urea from emitted CO2 from power plants and other sources. In addition, it has been applied to reduce NOx from diesel cars and flue gas into N2 and O2, without using any catalyst, SCR or LNT. The anion-processors could remove NOx, about 95%.

We aim to design, demonstrate, produce, license our novel systems to customers in various industries to clean the sky. We are seeking partnerships to implement the commercialization and demonstration of this novel clean technology.
